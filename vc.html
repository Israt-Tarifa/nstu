Vice Chancellor

Professor Dr. Md. Didar-ul-Alam

Professor Dr. Md. Didar-ul-Alam has been appointed as the Vice Chancellor (VC) (Second Period) of Noakhali University of Science and Technology (NSTU). He is a Retired professor in the Department of Soil, Water and Environment of Dhaka University. His Excellency the President and Chancellor of the University Mr. Mohammed Shahabuddin has given this appointment for the next four years as per Section 10 (1) of NSTU Act 2001.On Sunday 13 August 2023, with the approval of His Excellency the President and the Chancellor, the Secondary and Higher Education Department of the Ministry of Education issued a notification in this regard. This order will be implemented immediately.The Vice-Chancellor shall be permanently stationed on the University campus as the Chief Executive Officer of the University.
